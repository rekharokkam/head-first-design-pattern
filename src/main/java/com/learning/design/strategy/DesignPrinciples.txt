Definition
----------
Defines family of algorithms, encapsulates each one, and makes them interchangeable. Strategy lets the algorithm vary independently from the client that uses it.

Design Principle
----------------

1) Encapsulate what varies
2) Program to Interface and not to implementhation
3) Favor Composition over inheritance.

OO Basics
---------

1) Abstraction
2) Encapsulation
3) Polymorphism
4) Inheritance
